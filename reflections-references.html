Reflections & References

Blog Development - Reflection

I worked closely with Ildi throughout the development of the blog to maintain a consistent visual style and functionality. We regularly shared progress, discussed design decisions, and reviewed each other’s work to identify issues early. This helped us keep a unified aesthetic while dividing tasks efficiently. For example, while I focused on debugging the homepage and refining the rocket movement, Ildi developed the content pages to document the project.

Game Development - Reflection

Feedback encouraged us to integrate Granite more directly into gameplay so it would influence player decisions rather than act only as a coding assistant. Ideas included using Granite to start the spaceship or plan routes between planets.

Granite was used to generate early movement and interaction scripts, providing a strong foundation that still required manual refinement to fit the project structure. Overall, it accelerated development but still needed human adjustment for accuracy and integration.

Granite was also implemented as an in-game chatbot using a RAG system, allowing players to ask questions, receive hints, and access lore-based responses, making it part of the gameplay experience.

The project involved shared responsibilities across blog development, Unity integration, LLM systems, and UI design. While communication was generally effective, more consistent cross-team testing and check-ins would have improved coordination.

Overall, the project demonstrated both the strengths and limitations of Granite, showing it is most effective when combined with human oversight.
